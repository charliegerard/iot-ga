What is the Internet of Things?


Giant network of connected 'things'.

What does it even mean?

Example of devices

Nest

Thermostat: Change temperature depending on the current temperature in your house.

Smoke detector: Send text to your phone if fire in your house.

Amazon Echo

Voice commands to control “smart” appliances in your house. Like “ok google” for your house.

The Ninja Sphere

Device reacting to hand movement or touch to interact with your lights or music system.

SmartThings

Home appliances such as motion detector that can send you notification if someone enters your house or turn on the lights when you’re home.

WunderBar

LittleBits

Stuff you could do?

Arduino

Starter kit with Arduino UNO, breadboard, buttons, LEDs, jumper wires, capacitors, etc...

Stuff you could do?
Your own remote controlled robot

Raspberry Pi

Credit-card sized computer that can browser the Interet, play games, make spreadsheets, etc...

Stuff you could do?
Game console with 100 old-school games
Automatic cat feeder

Grove starter Kit by Intel

IoT starter kit working with the Intet Galileo or Edison including buzzers, buttons, LED, touch sensor, smart relay, sound sensor, etc...

Stuff you could do?
